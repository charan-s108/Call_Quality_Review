detectMoments

detectMoments.js · pure function
  • Escalation — cancel, refund, manager, lawsuit, ridiculous, unacceptable
  • Empathy — /i.*understand/i, /i.?m sorry/i, I apologise, I can see why
  • Dead Air — gap between turns > 15 s
  • Long Monologue — turn word count > 50
turns[ ] → moments[ ] · sorted by priority desc

computeSummary

summary.js · calls coaching.js
  • talkRatio — agent vs customer turn %
  • escalationCount — filtered moments
  • empathyScore — empathy / (empathy + escalation)
  • sentimentArc — first-half vs second-half escalations → improved / declined / neutral
  • momentDensity — total moments / duration (min)
  • coachingNote — rule-based text

generateCoachingNote

coaching.js · deterministic rules
  • Escalation + no empathy → acknowledge earlier
  • Empathy after escalation → still too late
  • ≥ 2 dead-air periods → review hold workflow
  • 1 dead-air → avoid extended silences
  • Long monologue (customer) → clarify earlier
  • Long monologue (agent) → keep concise
  • Empathy, no escalation → strong empathy
No AI / LLM — explainable & auditable
